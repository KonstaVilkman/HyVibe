# Hytale Vibe / Hangout Server — Vision Document

**Project Codename:** Vibe  
**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** January 2026  
**Status:** Pre-Development / Planning

---

## Executive Summary

A social sandbox platform built on Hytale, designed for players who want to chill, connect, and express themselves. Think of it as a cozy MMO-lite meets digital hangout space — a place where progression feels rewarding without pressure, and every corner invites you to stay a while.

**Core Promise:** A trusted, non-pay-to-win social platform where players can relax, roleplay, customize their space, and build meaningful connections.

## Core Pillars

### 1. Social Hub
The heart of the server — a central gathering space designed for organic interaction.

- **Central Plaza/Town:** Main spawn area with cafes, parks, fountains, benches
- **Themed Districts:** Different vibes (cozy village, neon city, nature retreat)
- **Public Venues:** Concert stages, movie theaters, community centers
- **Ambient NPCs:** Background characters that make the world feel alive

### 2. Player Housing
Personal space for self-expression and creativity.

- **Plot System:** Players claim and own personal land
- **Building Freedom:** Decorate interiors and exteriors
- **Furniture & Items:** Collectible decor from events, shops, achievements
- **Visiting:** Players can tour each other's homes
- **Neighborhoods:** Grouped housing areas with community identity

### 3. AFK Progression / Idle Systems
Rewarding players for simply existing in the world.

- **Passive XP:** Earn experience just by being online
- **Idle Resources:** Gather materials over time (garden growth, fishing spots)
- **Loyalty Rewards:** Daily login bonuses, streak rewards
- **AFK Zones:** Designated chill spots with bonus idle rewards
- **Cosmetic Unlocks:** Progression tied to appearance items, not power

### 4. Minigames
Casual, fun activities for spontaneous play.

- **Parkour Courses:** Timed runs with leaderboards
- **Fishing:** Relaxing activity with rare catches
- **Trivia/Quiz Nights:** Scheduled community events
- **Racing:** Foot races, boat races, obstacle courses
- **Hide & Seek:** Classic social game
- **Rhythm/Music Games:** If Hytale supports it
- **Seasonal Events:** Holiday-themed minigames

### 5. Roleplay Systems
Tools for players to craft identity and narrative.

- **Jobs/Professions:** Barista, gardener, shopkeeper, DJ, tour guide
- **Economy:** Server currency earned through jobs and activities
- **Character Identity:** Bios, titles, custom name tags
- **Roleplay Zones:** Designated areas for immersive RP
- **Relationship Systems:** Friend lists, guild/group mechanics

### 6. Events & Atmosphere
Making the world feel alive and worth returning to.

- **Seasonal Events:** Halloween, Winter, Summer festivals
- **Live Events:** Concerts, community gatherings, grand openings
- **Day/Night Cycle:** Ambient lighting changes
- **Weather Systems:** Rain, snow, sunshine affecting mood
- **Ambient Music:** Chill lo-fi, nature sounds, themed area music
- **Dynamic NPCs:** Characters that react to time, weather, events

## Monetization Framework

### Guiding Principles

1. **Never Pay-to-Win:** No gameplay advantages for money
2. **Cosmetics First:** Revenue from appearance, not power
3. **Transparent Pricing:** No hidden fees, clear value
4. **Trust is Currency:** Player trust is more valuable than short-term revenue
5. **Diversified Income:** Don't rely solely on player spending

---

### Revenue Stream 1: In-Server (Direct from Players)

| Product | Description | Price Range |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **Cosmetic Packs** | Outfits, furniture sets, pets, particles | $2 - $15 |
| **Season Pass** | Free + Premium track with exclusive cosmetics | $5 - $10/season |
| **VIP Subscription** | Monthly perks (extra plots, titles, emotes) | $5 - $10/month |
| **Housing Expansions** | Larger plots, additional plots | $3 - $10 |
| **One-Time Bundles** | Themed collections (Cozy Cabin Pack, Neon Pack) | $10 - $25 |

**What VIP Includes (Example):**
- Exclusive cosmetics (monthly)
- Extra housing plot
- Custom title/badge
- Priority event access
- XP boost (cosmetic progression only)
- Exclusive AFK zones

**What VIP Does NOT Include:**
- Gameplay advantages
- Pay-to-skip content
- Exclusive minigame access
- Economy advantages
