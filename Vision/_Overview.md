## Quick Summary

**HyVibe** is a cozy social/hangout Hytale server where players can relax, roleplay, customize their space, and build meaningful connections.

### Core Promise
> A trusted, non-pay-to-win social platform where your time investment is always respected.

### Vision Statement
> "A place that feels like home — where you can be yourself, meet friends, and always have something chill to do."

---

## Core Pillars

1. **Social Hub** — Central gathering spaces for organic interaction
2. **Player Housing** — Personal space for self-expression
3. **AFK Progression** — Rewarding players for existing in the world
4. **Minigames** — Casual, fun activities for spontaneous play
5. **Roleplay Systems** — Tools for crafting identity and narrative
6. **Events & Atmosphere** — Making the world feel alive

---

## Target Audience

- Social Gamers (hanging out > competition)
- Casual Players (low-pressure, drop-in)
- Roleplayers (identity, jobs, narrative)
- Decorators/Creators (customizing spaces)
- AFK/Idle Fans (passive progression)
- Community Seekers (welcoming online home)

**Age Range:** 13+ (family-friendly)

---

## Non-Negotiable Principles

| Principle | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| No Pay-to-Win | Money buys cosmetics, never power |
| Player Trust First | Transparent systems, no manipulation |
| Fair Progression | Time investment is respected |
| Cozy, Not Grindy | Rewarding without pressure |
