# Crystals

Economy anchor item - tradeable, used for parties and housing features.

---

## Overview

Crystals drop from lootboxes only. They're fully tradeable and serve as the backbone of player-to-player trading.

---

## Uses

| Use | Cost |
|-----|------|
| Small Party | 1 Crystal |
| Big Party | 2 Crystals |
| Mega Party | 4 Crystals |
| Balcony Unlock | 10-15 Crystals |
| Rooftop Access | 15-20 Crystals |
| Secret Room | 25-35 Crystals |
