# Plant Seeds

Seeds for the passive lootbox generation system in apartments.

---

## Seed Types

| Seed | Grow Time | Yield |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Common Seed | 12 hours | 1 Common Lootbox |
| Rare Seed | 48 hours | 1 Rare Lootbox |
| Epic Seed | 72 hours | 1 Epic Lootbox |
| Super Seed | 72 hours | 1 Epic + bonus chance |
